# 🛒 E-Commerce Data Analysis using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an E-commerce dataset using SQL to derive meaningful business insights. The analysis covers customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and inventory management. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by uncovering patterns and opportunities within the data.

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of the following tables:

* **Customers**: Contains customer details such as ID, name, and location
* **Orders**: Includes order-level information like order date and total amount
* **Order_Details**: Contains product-level transaction details (quantity, price)
* **Products**: Includes product information such as category and product name

---

## 🔍 Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Checked for missing (NULL) values in key columns
* Identified and filtered negative or invalid values
* Detected outliers in pricing
* Converted date fields into proper date format
* Validated total order amount consistency

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Customer Insights (Customer Behavior Analysis)

* Identified frequent and occasional customers
* Analyzed high and low spenders
* Calculated customer recency
* Segmented customers based on purchase frequency
* Evaluated customer distribution by location
* Analyzed new customer acquisition trends

---

### 🔹 Product Analysis (Product Performance)

* Determined best-selling and least-selling products
* Analyzed category-wise performance
* Evaluated product demand across locations
* Identified products with low customer engagement
* Measured product revenue contribution

---

### 🔹 Sales Optimization (Trend Analysis)

* Analyzed daily sales trends
* Calculated month-on-month growth rate
* Evaluated location-wise sales performance
* Analyzed average order value fluctuations
* Identified peak sales periods

---

### 🔹 Inventory Management

* Analyzed product demand patterns
* Tracked monthly inventory movement
* Identified high-demand products requiring frequent restocking
* Evaluated category-wise inventory distribution

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* A small group of customers contributes significantly to overall revenue
* Certain product categories consistently outperform others
* Sales show clear monthly trends, indicating seasonal demand
* Some products have low engagement and may require marketing focus or removal
* High-demand products need optimized inventory planning to avoid stockouts

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Focus on retaining high-value customers through targeted campaigns
* Promote underperforming products with discounts or bundling strategies
* Optimize inventory for high-demand products
* Leverage seasonal trends for marketing and sales planning
* Expand in high-performing locations
